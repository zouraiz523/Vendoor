Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise plans, we also accept wire transfers.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Your billing will be prorated based on your usage.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime with no penalties.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for registered non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit status.

What happens if I exceed my product limit?

If you exceed your product limit, you won't be able to add more products until you upgrade your plan. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit.
